# Product FAQ (Excerpt)

## Billing
- Invoices are generated on the first business day of each month.
- Refunds are processed within 7 to 10 business days.

## Integrations
- SSO supports SAML 2.0 and OIDC providers.
- Webhooks retry up to 3 times on 5xx failures.

## Data Retention
- Default retention is 365 days.
- Enterprise plans can request custom retention.
