# Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

Welcome to Acme Corp. This handbook summarizes key policies and onboarding
steps for new employees.

## Onboarding
- Complete ID verification on your first day.
- Submit payroll details in the HR portal within 5 business days.
- Engineering staff must request VPN access and a production read-only role.

## Time Off
- Standard paid leave is 20 days per year.
- Sick leave can be used for personal or family illness.
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 7 days in advance.

## Security
- All employees must enable multi-factor authentication.
- Do not share credentials or store passwords in plain text.
- Report suspected phishing within 2 hours to security@acme.example.

## Remote Work
- Core collaboration hours are 10:00 to 16:00 local time.
- Equipment stipends are available once per year.
