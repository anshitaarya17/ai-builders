# Engineering Runbook (Excerpt)

## Incident Response
1. Acknowledge the alert within 5 minutes.
2. Declare severity using the SEV rubric.
3. Open a Zoom bridge and post updates every 15 minutes.

## Rollback Policy
- If error rate exceeds 3% for 10 minutes, rollback is required.
- Notify the incident commander before rollback.

## Customer Impact
- Document affected endpoints and user segments.
- Provide a timeline in the incident report within 48 hours.

## Postmortems
- Draft a post-incident review within 5 business days.
- Track action items in the reliability backlog.
